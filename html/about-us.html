Welcome to FlashFocus! A new and powerful tool to highlight PDFs to further understand concepts. FlashFocus was started by two high school students, Eddie Bian and Ilya Jahromi, who found how hard it is to export highlights from standard PDFs. Assisting students all around the world to study faster and smarter by exporting their highlights with just a single click of a button! At FlashFocus, we're committed to simplifying the studying process and empowering learners to excel in their academic pursuits. Our innovative platform not only streamlines the task of highlighting essential information but also offers advanced features to enhance your study experience. With customizable highlighting options, annotation tools, and seamless integration with your favorite devices, FlashFocus provides a comprehensive solution for students of all levels.
